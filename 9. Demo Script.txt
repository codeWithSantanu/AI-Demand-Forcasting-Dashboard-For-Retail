🟢 C. DEMO SCRIPT (EXACT WORDS TO SPEAK)

---

🟢 0:00 – 0:30 → HOOK (PROBLEM)

👉 Speak confidently:

> “In India, millions of citizens miss out on government benefits because they don’t know which schemes they are eligible for or how to apply.”

> “The information is scattered across multiple websites and often difficult to understand.”

---

🟡 0:30 – 1:30 → LIVE DEMO (MOST IMPORTANT 🔥)

👉 Open your application

Say:

> “Here, the user enters basic details such as age, income, category, and available documents.”

👉 Enter sample data:

Age: 22
Income: 200000
Category: unemployed
Documents: aadhaar

👉 Click Check Eligibility

---

👉 While showing results:

Say:

> “The system instantly displays all eligible government schemes.”

👉 Point to screen:

“This is the scheme name”
“This is the readiness score indicating how prepared the user is”
“These are the missing documents required to apply”
“And this is the official link for application”

👉 Click link (very important)

---

🔵 1:30 – 2:00 → RESULT (VALUE)

Say:

> “Instead of spending hours searching, users can now identify relevant schemes within seconds along with clear guidance on what they need to apply.”

> “This improves accessibility and awareness of government benefits.”

---

🟣 2:00 – 2:30 → TECHNICAL BUILD

Say:

> “We built this system using React for the frontend and Node.js for the backend. The system uses a structured dataset and a scoring algorithm to determine eligibility and readiness.”

👉 Optional:

> “We also integrated AI to simplify scheme explanations.”

---

🔴 2:30 – 3:00 → FUTURE SCOPE / CLOSING

Say:

> “In the future, we plan to expand this system by adding more schemes, multilingual support, and real-time integration with government platforms.”

👉 Strong closing line:

> “Our goal is to make government schemes accessible to every citizen in a simple and transparent way.”
