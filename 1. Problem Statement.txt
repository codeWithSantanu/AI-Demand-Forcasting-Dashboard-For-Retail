📌 A. INTRODUCTION (FOR REPORT)

In India, the government provides numerous welfare schemes aimed at improving the lives of citizens, especially those belonging to economically weaker sections, unemployed individuals, students, and farmers. However, despite the availability of these schemes, a large portion of the population remains unaware of the benefits they are eligible for.

The information regarding these schemes is scattered across multiple government websites and portals, often presented in complex and technical language. As a result, users find it difficult to identify relevant schemes, understand eligibility criteria, and complete the application process efficiently.

---

🚨 B. CORE PROBLEM DESCRIPTION

The major issues faced by users include:

Lack of awareness about available government schemes
Difficulty in determining eligibility based on personal criteria
Complex and non-user-friendly government portals
Absence of a centralized system for scheme discovery
Time-consuming manual search process

👉 This leads to:

Missed financial and social benefits
Reduced participation in welfare programs
Increased dependency on intermediaries

🧾 E. DETAILED PROBLEM STATEMENT (FOR PROJECT REPORT)

> In India, a wide range of government schemes are introduced to support citizens in areas such as housing, healthcare, education, and employment. However, the awareness and accessibility of these schemes remain limited, particularly among unemployed and economically weaker sections. The lack of a centralized and user-friendly system makes it difficult for individuals to determine which schemes they qualify for. Additionally, complex eligibility criteria and scattered information across multiple platforms increase the difficulty of accessing these benefits. This results in underutilization of government initiatives and loss of potential financial support. Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent system that simplifies scheme discovery, evaluates eligibility, and guides users through the application process effectively.
